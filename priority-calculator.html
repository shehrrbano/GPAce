Priority Score Formula

Credit Hours (CHP)
Reflects subject importance based on credit hours.
CHP = Relative Score (0-100)
Cognitive Diff (CDP)
Measures task complexity and intellectual challenge.
CDP = Cog Diff (1-100)
Task Weightage (TWP)
Indicates the importance of the task within its section.
TWP = Avg Weightage (0-100)
Time Remaining (TRP)
Dynamically adjusts priority based on task deadline.
Upc: 100 * (1/(Days + Hrs/24)) Ovd: 100 * (1+log(Days+1))
Acad Perf Adj (APA)
Higher overall subject performance reduces priority.
APA = Subject Perf (0-100)
Final Priority Score
APA acts as a percentage reduction of the total score.
Score = (CHP+CDP+TWP+TRP) × (1 - APA/100)
